A Special Note For You ❤️

Dear madam ji,

I still remember 15 June, around 10:30 PM—the moment your first message appeared. It became the beginning of something I'll always remember.

Before you came into my life, everything felt a little dull. I smiled, I laughed, I lived... but something always felt missing. Then you came like a little ray of light, and somehow everything started feeling brighter. You didn't just enter my life—you brought a new kind of happiness that I never knew I needed.

Every conversation with you makes my day better. Every notification from you brings a smile to my face. You may not realize it, but your presence has changed me in the most beautiful way.

I don't know what the future holds, but I do know one thing—I want to create beautiful memories with you, make you smile when you're sad, stand beside you when life gets hard, and remind you every single day how special you are.

If this page has reached your heart even a little, then I'd like to ask you something...

Will you let me be the reason behind your smile, just as you've become the reason behind mine?

Thank you for becoming one of the most beautiful parts of my life.

From that first message on 15 June at 10:30 PM, to every moment that lies ahead, you'll always be special to me. ❤️
